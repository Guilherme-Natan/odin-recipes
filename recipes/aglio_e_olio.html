Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Steps to produce:
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until golden and fragrant. Add red pepper flakes if using.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine. If needed, add reserved pasta water a little at a time to loosen the sauce.
  4. Season with salt to taste, garnish with chopped parsley and Parmesan if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
